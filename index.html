The Problem

AWS Lambda doesn't offer GPU support. Developers with serverless architectures are forced to use more complex and expensive infrastructure like SageMaker or EC2 for GPU-dependent tasks, breaking the serverless paradigm and increasing costs.

The Solution

LambdaX offers a pay-per-use GPU compute platform that's deeply integrated with AWS. Migrate your existing Lambda projects with minimal code changes and slash your costs by leveraging our intelligent, high-density multi-tenant architecture.

Why LambdaX?

Effortless Migration

A true "lift-and-shift" experience. Keep your existing IAM roles and VPC networking. Just change your Docker base image, add `.to('cuda')` to your code, and deploy.

Drastic Cost Reduction

Our "Slice-Second" pricing model and intelligent scheduler ensure you pay only for the exact resources you use, for the exact time you use them. Stop paying for idle GPUs.

Secure & Isolated

We use advanced GPU partitioning to create hardware-isolated, secure slices for each workload. Your code and data run completely segregated from other tenants.

Familiar Developer Experience

With a `lambdaX-cli` and an API designed to mirror AWS Lambda, your team can onboard and deploy in minutes without learning a new ecosystem.

Seamless VPC Integration

Connect to your private resources like RDS databases and caches with our secure AWS PrivateLink-based networking features. No public internet exposure.

Intelligent Scheduling

Our secret sauce. The LambdaX scheduler solves a real-time "bin packing" problem to place workloads on warm workers, minimizing cold starts and maximizing GPU utilization to drive down costs.
